scrambled Eggs


Eggs are one of my favorite foods.here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using the spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second(as you are making the omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
